About the Majestic Lodge

This is not your ordinary Breckenridge mountain lodge.

Upon arrival, you’ll be greeted with a fresh, upscale, and modern design that Instagram dreams are made of. Gone are the stale, stuffy, mountain themes. Instead, the Majestic Lodge features pops of color paired against reclaimed wood features, lush linens, and ample, luxurious furnishings. It’s bright, clean, cozy, modern, and just the right amount of rustic—all in one unforgettable home.

With over 6,700 square feet of usable space, this spectacular mountain home located in the prestigious Breckenridge Highlands boasts ample space and amenities to make your getaway unforgettable. Accommodation is a breeze at the Lodge, as we provide a generous sleeping capacity for 21 people across 6 spacious bedrooms and more. So, whether you’re looking for plenty of space to gather, or enough privacy to unwind, the Lodge has just what you need.
